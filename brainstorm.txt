## COMMANDS

"die" -> shuts down computer, w/ "Y or N" confirmation prompt.

"vol xxx" -> sets volume to given percent/level.

"specs" -> lists computer specs

## TILING FUNCTIONALITY

A command like "gochrome (website) 1/8" opense chrome window with URL in that tile space.

Just "...1/4" will make tile occupy 2nd quadrant of screen (think unit circle)while  "...1/4v" would make tile vertically 1/4th.
